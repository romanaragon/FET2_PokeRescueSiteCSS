Tips on making sure your Pokemon is happy!

  1. Take your Pokemon with you as many places as you can so they don't get lonely.
  2. Make sure to socialize your Pokemon with other people and Pokemon.
  3. Have your Pokemon regularly checked up on at the PokeClinic by Nurse Joy
  4. If you see team Rocket, grab your Pokebaby and RUN AWAY!
  5. Most importantly, love your Pokemon with all your heart and give it a bite of your snacks.

How much food to give your Pokemon according to size

Small 1 pound
Medium 2 pounds
Large 3 pounds

How much water to give your Pokemon according to size

Small 16 ounces a day
Medium 32 ounces a day
Large 63 ounces a day
